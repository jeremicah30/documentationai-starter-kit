---
title: I can't find my project documentation
description: Recover or locate lost documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- Project docs missing from dashboard
- Pages or content not showing up

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Workspace">
    Ensure you're in the correct workspace or team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Projects">
    Use the search bar to find your project name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Permissions">
    Confirm you have access to the required documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
