## Organize Projects

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Project">
    Navigate to Projects > New Project and enter your project's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation Pages">
    Inside your new project, click 'Add Page' for guides, FAQs, or references.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange Pages">
    Drag and drop pages in the sidebar to reorder as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
